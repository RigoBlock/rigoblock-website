subtitle: "05. Principles"
slug: "project-05-principles"
---

- **Safety First**

  Continuous code reviews. 2 full-scope audits. Rules that prevent vault draining — even for fully autonomous agents.

- **Open by Default**

  Permissionless vault deployment and management. 100% Open Source.

- **Full Transparency**

  All vault operations, governance decisions, and incentive distributions happen on-chain and are publicly verifiable.

- **Aligned Incentives**

  Proof-of-Performance rewards operators and GRG stakers.

- **Agent-Native by Design**

  Scoped Delegation, X402 API, Human-in-the-Loop or Fully Autonomous modes.

- **Onchain bindings**

  Our vaults remove unnecessary token approvals. They block rogue transactions by design.

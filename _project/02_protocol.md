Rigoblock is a complete infrastructure stack for both humans and autonomous agents: on-chain vault contracts, and an off-chain layer with additional security guarantees.

You build the strategy, we provide the tools for your strategy to scale. Access multiple
chains in parallel, swap tokens, provide liquidity or hedge your strategies. All secured
by rules enforced at the protocol level.
